About Me

I'm an Automation & System Engineer with a passion for turning complex infrastructure into scalable, reliable, and fully automated platforms. Over the past decade — primarily in the financial industry — I've built and operated Linux-based environments and designed end-to-end automation solutions.

My work spans Terraform/OpenTofu provider and module development, Ansible & AAP automation, containerization, virtualization, and lifecycle management of large-scale Red Hat environments.

I enjoy creating clarity: standardised processes, clean architectures, strong documentation, and smooth collaboration across teams. Currently based in Zürich, Switzerland.
